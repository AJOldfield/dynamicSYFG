About Share Your Farming Gear




Share Your Farming Gear is a place for farmers and and contractors to match spare machinery capacity with jobs that need doing. If you need a job done, list it. If you want to start renting or contracting - even for a small window - list that too. Interested in sharing machinery ownership? We'll search for compatible partners. Worried about machinery availability? We will soon insure against that. Hate admin? We automate it!

Share Your Farming Gear exists to help Australian farmers and contractors increase profits through increased machinery utilisation and more efficient access to rental machinery and contractors. When the system is fully built it will have five main features:

  1. An efficient national marketplace for machinery rental and contractor hire
  2. Intelligent matching of farmers with compatible schedules who could profitably share machinery
  3. The provision of insurance to guarantee machinery availability
  4. Automated administration for all renting, sharing and contracting
  5. A market for sharing trusted labour.
