Current Features
Our website currently facilitates tendering for contracting work. When you list any work you need done, we'll quickly provide you offers from multiple contractors interested in working for you.

Coming Features
We currently have a small feature set and offering, reflecting the youth of our business. Over the next months they'll continue to grow:

  • We'll roll out our full account structure and searching functionality in the next few weeks.
  • We're working on providing simple one-click payment and invoicing for all services hired through us.
  • We've already begun building a database of trusted and skilled seasonal labour to offer for hire through our site.
  • We're in negotiations to allow us to provide cheap insurance for machinery hired through us.

As we're starting out we know we won't have every contractor listed, but when we aren't able to immediately provide you a selection of competitive offers we're committed to doing the legwork to find them. It will always be free to list work or services, so you'll only ever pay if you find an offer that you want to investigate. We hope you understand what we're providing and are interested in listing some work soon. If there's any way we could serve you better we'd love to hear from you.
